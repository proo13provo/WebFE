About the Game

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure RPG set in the megalopolis of Night City, where you play as a cyberpunk mercenary armed with cybernetic enhancements and build your legend on the streets of Night City.

Take the role of V — a hired gun on the rise — and step into a world where cyberware is the key to survival. Explore the vast city where the choices you make shape the story and the world around you.
